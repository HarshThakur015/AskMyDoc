HEALTH INSURANCE BENEFITS

We provide comprehensive health insurance through BlueCross BlueShield. The standard plan covers 80% of all in-network medical expenses after a $1,500 deductible is met. Dental and vision coverage are included at no additional cost for the employee. Dependents can be added to the policy during the open enrollment period in November each year. For emergency room visits, there is a flat copay of $250. Prescription medications fall under a three-tier system: $10 for generic, $35 for preferred brand name, and $60 for non-preferred medications. Mental health services are covered at the same rate as physical health services. Life insurance equal to two times annual salary is provided at no cost to the employee.
