REMOTE WORK POLICY

The company fully supports a hybrid remote work environment. Employees are expected to be in the office on Tuesdays and Thursdays. Monday, Wednesday, and Friday are designated as flexible remote days. Employees working remotely must ensure they have a stable internet connection of at least 50Mbps. If an employee relocates to a different state, they must notify Human Resources within 30 days due to tax implications. Our core working hours are 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM EST, during which all team members must be online and available for meetings. All remote employees must use VPN when accessing internal company systems. Expense reimbursements for home office equipment are capped at $500 per year.
